Lasagna

Classic Comfort Food

This is a recipe for lasagna.

I like lasagna.

It has meat and noodles and cheese.

What more could you want?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb beef
  • 1/2 lb pork
  • 1 tbsp each: oregano, basil, rosemary
  • 1/2 yellow onion chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves chopped, divided
  • 9 lasagna noodles boiled al dente
  • 2 cans of your favourite marinara sauce (roughly 48 oz)
  • 1 16 oz container ricotta
  • 4 tbsp pesto
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup parmesan shredded
  • 4-5 cups mozzarella shredded

Steps

  1. About an hour before cooking, spread out ricotta on a plate and place a couple paper towels over it to soak up some of the moisture.
  2. Preheat oven to 375.
  3. Prepare lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  4. In a skillet, crumble and cook beef, pork, onion, herbs and 2 diced garlic cloves.
  5. While beef is cooking, add 2 cans of marinara sauce to a large sauce pan and warm up.
  6. After beef and pork are cooked through, add to marinara sauce, do not drain.
  7. Simmer until you are ready to assemble lasagna.
  8. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, pesto, 2 diced garlic cloves, and parmesan cheese.
  9. In a 9x13 pan, spread a thin layer of sauce.
  10. Next, lay out your first layer to noodles and cover with meat sauce.
  11. Aadd a generous layer of mozzarella.
  12. Then another layer of noodles topped with the ricotta blend.
  13. Sprinkle with more mozzarella.
  14. One more layer of noodles and add the rest of the sauce... and don't put the last cheese layer on yet. Cover with foil, cut a few vents and bake for 30 minutes.
  15. Remove foil and add the last layer of mozzarella. Sprinkle on a little garlic salt and parmesan.
  16. Bake for another 20-30 minutes, uncovered, until the cheese is browned and bubbly.
